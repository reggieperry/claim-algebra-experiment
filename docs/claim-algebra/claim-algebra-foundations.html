The general form

Parameters

The credit application is one instantiation; the core is written against: a value type A with a lawful Eq[A] coinciding with universal ==; an idempotent merge policy rep : A→A (a quotient, never an ε-tolerance); an extensible Kind of citation sorts; a render carrier M, any lawful CommutativeRig[M] with Order[M]; a per-channel valuation (νpro, νcon); a threshold θ ∈ M; a verifier; and an acyclic topology of nodes.

Provenance — the free commutative semiring ℕ[X]

Lineage is a citation token (the alphabet X), now carrying a Kind. Monomial is a multiset of tokens with strictly-positive exponents (joint use, the product ·). Prov = ℕ[X] is a polynomial: monomials with ℕ coefficients (alternative derivation, the sum +); 0 = the gap, 1 = supported-citing-nothing, 0 ≠ 1. Coefficients and exponents are BigInt (fail-closed; FOLD 4). The grade is never stored — it is the polynomial evaluated by the unique homomorphism ν̂ into M.

The carrier — candidate maps (FOLD 1)

Testimony[A] = A →₀ (Prov, Prov) — a finite map from each candidate value to its (for, against) provenance, normalized to drop the all-zero pair. The single value is a derived read: present only at exactly one for-candidate with nothing against.

The four combiners

corroborate ⊕ₖ = pointwise (+,+) over the key union (a commutative monoid). derive ⊗ₖ = f-convolution with · on both channels (fail-closed: an empty channel annihilates). truthMeet ∧ₜ = · on for, the three-term cross-term on against (FOLD 1 — the refutation fork: a refuted conjunct → F, distinct from a gap's N). refute ¬ = per-candidate channel swap (involutive).

The corner and the gate (FOLD 2)

The Belnap corner is read structurally from the channels: N (both empty), T (for only), F (against only), B (both — a glut). Cardinality ≥ 2 for-candidates is ambiguous — a distinct, model-free read, not a fifth corner. The gate signs ⇔ corner = T ∧ cardinality = 1 ∧ grade ≥ θ ∧ verify; every other outcome is a block, none of them a confident error.

The relaxation table

This table is the relaxation contract for the general claim algebra, built from the verified theorem corpus (all VALID or CORRECTED) against the locked spec's four folds. Each of the 53 distinct algebraic properties below is tagged ESSENTIAL — relaxing it breaks internal consistency or opens a CWS hole — or RELAXABLE — a deliberate variation point or diagnostic — with the consequence of relaxing it and the guarding theorem and law-test suite.

The four folds

The four folds' invariants resolve as anticipated:

  • Fold 1. The truthMeet refutation FORK is ESSENTIAL (a refuted conjunct→F, distinct from a gap's N); its specific con-operation is RELAXABLE (two-term '+' and the three-term cross-term give the same fork corner; the cross-term additionally recovers F∧ₜF=F at the cost of an honest F∧ₜN=N divergence from the untouched Ev-level F∧ₜx=F).
  • Fold 2. cardinality=1 / Ambiguous-distinct-from-Glut is ESSENTIAL and only narrows acceptance (CWS tightened).
  • Fold 3. Ratio quantization / map-key soundness (Eq=universal ==) is ESSENTIAL; ε-closeness is banned.
  • Fold 4. ℕ[X] zerosumfree + integral + BigInt/checked fail-closed arithmetic is ESSENTIAL (a subtractive ring or silent Int overflow flips corners fail-OPEN).

The structural spine and the variation points

The structural spine is uniformly ESSENTIAL: Monomial/Prov canonicality and CommutativeRig laws, the independent annihilator 0·x=0, GKT existence+uniqueness of ν̂ with ν̂(0)=0, the carrier-as-CommutativeRig-with-absorbing-zero, Lev as a fail-closed total order / bounded distributive lattice, Ev as the distributive bilattice Lev⊙Lev (twist), N⊗ₖx=N, gap≠glut, per-channel render compatibility, the model-free structural corner, the gate biconditional, and CWS asymmetry.

The genuine variation points are RELAXABLE: the render carrier (Lev|Viterbi|Count, the trust-model degree of freedom), the kind refinement (conservative over the Extraction-only default), the κ̂/rad/multiplicity sidecars (reads, never gate inputs), conflict-kind routing, and the rendered-corner=structural-corner equality (false as stated, harmless because the algebra reads only the structural corner).

Five honest gaps, carried in the table not hidden
